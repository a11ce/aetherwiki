Antipressure Front

The Antipressure Front is an activist group with the goal of ending all industrial aethercompression. They are known to be active at the Nordovest Institute. Many of their members are wizards.

Antipressure Front Itaan is a related radical branch that split off from the Nordovest group in the 360s. They are designated as an enemy of the state by Arkand.
