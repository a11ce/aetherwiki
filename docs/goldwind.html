Goldwind Agricultural Cooperative

Goldwind Agricultural Cooperative is a Tilian farmer cooperative specializing in the production of crop dusters equipment from used low-grade bulk cartridges.

Before the founding of Goldwind, individual farms would commonly hire wizards to spray fertilizer and pest repellent.

Founded in 458 by 4 farms that purchase a triplex compressor to refill low-grade skystone tanks. Use planes to spray fertilizer/repellent. See Locust War.
