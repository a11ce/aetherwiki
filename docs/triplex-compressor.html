Triplex Compressor

A triplex compressor is a stationary, building-sized aethercompression pump. They were invented in 346 in Arkandic engineer Goran Feldt and became the standard depot compressor after the Triplex Crisis led to the nationalization of the patent.

They compress to blue-grade density.
