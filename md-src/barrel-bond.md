---
title: "Barrel Bonds"
tag: Politics
---

Barrel bonds are a recovery scheme by [[IPRO]] and [[Goldwind]] after the [[Locust War]]. IPRO sells Tilia blue barrels at market price but waives tax. Tilia takes investments to buy them and then offers redeemable bonds on individual barrels. [[Pantheon|finance guild]] is the largest single owner of barrel bonds.
