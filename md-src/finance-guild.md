The **Finance Guild** is a national syndicate of [[Arkand]] responsible for investment, taxation, and financial policy.

The Guild recieved the [[triplex compressor]] patent during the [[triplex crisis]] and licenses it internationally.

Its foreign investment arm, Pantheon, owns many [[barrel bonds|barrel bond]] and, since 434, [[Lock 19|lock 19 blockade]] in [[Liara]].
