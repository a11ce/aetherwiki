The **Fox River** flows east from the western [[Kota]] mountains to the [[Laumeri]]. It forms the southern border of [[Tilia]], separating it from Kota and [[Ikara]]. Herds of [[sneals|sneal]] migrate seasonally between the [[White River Forest]] and the eastern delta of the Fox River.
